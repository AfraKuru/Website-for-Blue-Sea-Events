ABOUT US

We want YOU to celebrate.

At Blue Sea Events, we believe every celebration should feel effortless, personal, and truly unforgettable. Based in Cumming, Georgia, we proudly serve clients throughout Metro Atlanta, bringing thoughtful design and seamless coordination to events of all kinds.

Blue Sea Events was founded by a mother with a deep passion for creating meaningful moments, alongside her two daughters who share her love for creativity, detail, and celebration. Together, we are a family-run team dedicated to turning your vision into a beautifully executed experience — so you can relax, be present, and enjoy every moment that matters most.

OUR SERVICES

Thoughtful planning, seamless execution.

At Blue Sea Events, we take a calm, intentional approach to event planning, focusing on clear communication, thoughtful design, and attention to detail every step of the way. Whether you’re planning a wedding, celebration, or intimate gathering, our goal is to make the process feel effortless and enjoyable from start to finish.

If you have questions or would like to learn more, you’re always welcome to reach out to us directly via email. You can also submit a booking request through our website, and we’ll get back to you through your preferred method of communication within 1–2 business days.

To ensure availability and allow ample time for planning, we kindly ask that you contact us at least one month prior to your event date. Availability is limited, so early inquiries are encouraged.
